Port Whisperer scans your running processes and identifies development servers, automatically labeling them with their project names and frameworks. Never wonder "what's running on port 3000?" again.

It supports automatic detection of Next.js, Vite, Express, Django, and Rails dev servers, with a clean terminal UI showing all active ports at a glance.
